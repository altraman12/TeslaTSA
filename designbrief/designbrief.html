Augmented Reality has a wide variety of applications, both in today's society and tommorrow's. Every field, from Medicine to Gaming, has a need to present information to users more concisely. AR devices are uniquely suited to this task, and as a result they are gaining a lot of traction in each application. Learn about other applications here!
