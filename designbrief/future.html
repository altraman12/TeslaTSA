Future of UAS Tech

Surveying/Inspection

In the future, drones could play an integral part in surveying areas normally looked at by humans. This would save time and money, as well as reduce risk for a surveying team. A geographical area can be monitored for changes, crops can also be followed on the individual level. Thermal imaging will also allow for the identification of pipeline leakage among other uses. Surveying is a necessary component in many industries and drones wil play a key role in this field in the near future.

Military

The military has employed the use of drones in the past, but as technology advances, drones will play a deeper role in the military. Drones are readily becoming more potent and enable autonomous combat. This may radically change the way battles are fought in the future, relying not on men, but machines. Outside of war, drones may also be used by the army for surveillance, and may become useful tools for search and rescue missions.
